rewrite gap-closure vs B2 exceeds 100%. Does not yet meet the full pre-registered
margin (rewrite SD gate; dsl_learn barely passes). Not a kill: signal is consistent
enough to warrant extended training and/or additional seeds before revision or scale.

Confounds checked: identical data/tokens/steps; answer-suffix supervision; same lr
recipe. Remaining: dsl_learn sits at marginal validity from EXP-000C; absolute
accuracies are low (models still learning).
