# EXP-005: V3 selection — delta-density sweep (loops excluded per H3 park)

- Hypothesis: H6, redesigned 2026-07-05 after EXP-006 attribution (delta-dominant)
  and EXP-003B (H3 parked). V3 is delta-centric; the remaining design axis is
  delta-layer density.
- Date: pre-registered 2026-07-05, before any results
- Status: CONFIGURED (EXP-005-DEN arm)

## Design

V3-d1 (delta_every=1) and V3-d3 (delta_every=3) on algo_exec, standard protocol
(4000 steps, lr 4.9e-4), seeds 0-1. Existing cells: d2 = V2-delta (EXP-002-AX,
3 seeds, mean .965), B2 (.747). Param match: d1 17.88M / d2 17.86M / d3 17.85M
(all within 0.3% of B2's 17.83M, verified pre-launch).

## Pre-registered selection rule

- A density displaces d2 as V3 only if its 2-seed algo_exec mean beats d2's
  3-seed mean (.965) by >= 1.5 pts; ties broken by flops/correct.
- Otherwise d2 (the EXP-002 configuration) is confirmed as V3 by default.
- Secondary read: flops/correct ordering across densities (d3 is cheapest per
  token; if d3 ~= d2 on accuracy it may win the efficiency framing).
- The winner is then subject to the EXP-007 budget-robustness verdict before
  any 50M spend.

## Cost

4 jobs (CPU-bound), interleaved in the EXP-007 session.

## Mid-run deviation (2026-07-05, logged at decision time)

d1 arm DROPPED after throughput discovery: V3-d1 trains at 5.4k tok/s (2x the
sequential-scan cost of d2's 10.8k — delta on every layer doubles the
Python-bound scan work), i.e. ~5h/job at this protocol. Two consequences,
decided before any d1 eval results existed (seed 0 was at step ~600):
1. Cost: the d1 arm alone would exceed the whole session's budget envelope.
2. Substance: the pre-registered tie-break is flops/correct; d1 doubles
   training AND inference delta cost, so with d2 already at .965 (near
   ceiling), d1 is a dominated design point unless it matched d2's accuracy at
   HALF the cost — impossible by construction.
The sweep proceeds as d3-vs-d2 (d3 is the cheaper-than-d2 direction, which is
the direction that could actually win on the efficiency framing).

## Results

d3 arm 2/2 clean (session 3, 2026-07-05): V3-d3 algo_exec .935 / .940
(mean .938), flops/correct 8.27e9. Reference d2 (V2-delta): .965 mean,
8.0e9 flops/correct.

## Verdict

**d2 CONFIRMED as V3.** d3 is 2.7 pts worse AND ~3% more expensive per correct
answer (cheaper per token, but the accuracy denominator eats it) — it loses on
both the displacement rule (needed +1.5 over d2) and the efficiency tie-break.
d1 was dropped as dominated (see deviation note). The V3 "Aware" configuration
is therefore the EXP-002 design: 6L, delta_every=2, window 128, d_k=d_v=384,
17.86M params — now with budget-robustness (EXP-007) and mechanism attribution
(EXP-006) behind it.
