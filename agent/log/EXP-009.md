# EXP-009: grokking predictor — can first-1000-step signals forecast the rule_shift transition?

- Hypothesis: H7 (new, backlog) — early-training diagnostics separate
  grokked-to-be seeds from flat seeds before the transition.
- Motivation: EXP-002-RS EXTEND found a bimodal outcome on rule_shift: 1/6
  V2-delta seeds jumped to 100% eval accuracy (train loss 0.0014) while 5/6 sat
  at ~.14; 0/6 for B2. Capability-emergence forecasting in miniature.
- Date: pre-registered 2026-07-06, BEFORE any results.
- Status: PRE-REGISTERED

## Design

16 FRESH V2-delta seeds (14-29; originally 6-21, revised per the deviation log
below) on rule_shift, standard protocol (4000 x 32 x 768, lr 4.9e-4) — config
byte-identical to EXP-002's V2-delta (config_hash must match V2-delta's current
hash 03995cad7e6e at launch; the model is unchanged). The fresh seeds are
disjoint from EXP-002-RS seeds 0-5 so the rate estimate is independent;
existing seeds are NOT double-counted (results.csv dedupe rule).
Bonus job: seed 2 re-run WITH diagnostics (labeled `EXP-009 ... s2`, EXCLUDED
from the rate estimate — it is a diagnostics-capture attempt on a known-grokked
seed; hardware nondeterminism may or may not reproduce the transition, and
either outcome is informative about trajectory sensitivity but not about rate).

Diagnostics (train/diagnostics.py, opt-in `--diag`, JSONL sidecar per run under
checkpoints/diag/ — results.csv stays an eval-only ledger):
- light, every 25 steps to step 1000 then every 100: windowed train loss, loss
  curvature (2nd difference of the windowed series), grad-norm mean/max.
- heavy, every 200 steps: per-tier mini-eval on a PROBE set (10/tier from unused
  train-range seeds 900k+ — disjoint from training file AND eval split; greedy,
  16-token budget), weight norms (total / delta-layers / attn-layers),
  delta-state stats on a fixed probe batch (per-layer S Frobenius norm,
  effective rank = exp entropy of squared singular values, mean alpha/beta gates).
- checkpoints every 1000 steps (kept on pod; grokked runs' checkpoints pulled).
- Diagnostics NEVER influence training (no early stopping / selection; probe
  instances pre-generated so the training RNG stream is untouched).

## Definitions (fixed now)

- **Grokked** run: final eval all-tier accuracy >= 0.9 (final eval = the
  standard results.csv row). **Flat**: <= 0.3. Anything between is reported as
  "intermediate" and excluded from the Q2 contrast (but counted in Q1 verbatim).
- **Transition step**: first heavy record with probe all-tier accuracy >= 0.5.
- **Predictor window**: steps <= 1000, and strictly before the run's transition
  step if it transitions earlier (window then ends at the last heavy record
  before the transition; logged per run).

## Pre-registered questions and reads

- **Q1 (reproduction rate)**: k grokked out of 16 fresh seeds; report k/16 with
  Wilson 95% CI next to the prior point estimate 1/6. Descriptive — no gate, no
  margin. Also reported: transition-step distribution.
- **Q2 (early predictor)**: candidate signals, fixed now:
  1. loss curvature trajectory (windowed 2nd difference),
  2. grad-norm trajectory (mean and max),
  3. delta-state Frobenius norm growth rate,
  4. delta-state effective-rank trajectory,
  5. forget/write gate drift (mean alpha, mean beta),
  6. weight-norm ratio (delta-layers / attn-layers),
  7. earliest tier-1 probe accuracy lift.
  Read: for each signal, compare grokked vs flat groups over the predictor
  window. A signal is a **candidate predictor** only if it separates the groups
  PERFECTLY with k >= 2 grokked runs AND the group-mean gap exceeds 2x the flat
  group SD. Everything else is descriptive. With expected k ~ 2-4 this is
  hypothesis-generating, not confirmatory — stated in the note either way.
- If k = 0: Q2 is void; the note reports the rate CI and the diagnostic
  distributions (still useful as the null reference), plus whether the seed-2
  re-run transitioned.

## Cost

was for non-instrumented seeds at higher parallelism), 3 workers interleaved
with EXP-004's GPU lane across sessions (+1 bonus seed-2 job). Owner directive
2026-07-07: the ~$15 arc budget is a target, not a cap — the full 16-seed set
runs regardless; no seed trimming for budget.
